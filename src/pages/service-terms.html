Terms of Service Agreement.

By placing any service request order with Blue Water Legal Process LLC, you are entering into a legally binding agreement service contract and agree to be bound by the following terms of service:

Blue Water Legal Process LLC with make 3-4 service attempts of your legal documents: one morning, one afternoon, one evening attempt and one weekend Saturday attempt. The first service attempt will be started normally within 24-48 hours after confirmation of receiving your service documents. If the service of the documents cannot be completed within these attempts or Blue Water Legal Process LLC gains other information where the service cannot be completed, Blue Water Legal Process LLC will refer back to you before issuing a Non-Service affidavit.

Blue Water Legal Process LLC will be proactive in all these circumstances and will update you as needed. You agree to hold Blue Water Legal Process LLC harmless if these circumstances were to arise.
