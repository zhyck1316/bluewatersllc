About Blue Water Legal Process LLC.

Proud member of
National Association of Professional Process servers

Our Expertise

Blue Water Legal Process LLC serves the Blue Water Area of the State of Michigan. We service all types of legal process from Summons & Complaint, Subpoenas, Complaint for Divorce, Family Court Documents, Personal Protection Orders, Judgment Liens, Garnishments and much more.

Our Efficiency

We understand that when serving legal process, time is of the upmost importance and we pride ourselves on our ability to provide fast, friendly, effective Service. Being a Veteran owned business, we pride ourselves on that special attention to detail and ensure your serve provides a bullet-proof certificate of service that will hold up in court.

Our Professionalism

Blue Water Legal Process LLC adheres to the highest standards of professionalism and ethics in all of our operations. We are a proud member in good standing of the National Association of Professional Process Servers (Member #11923)

Each Service Request comes with the following
  • 3-4 service attempts (morning, afternoon, evening and weekend attempts)
  • Date/Time/GPS stamped photos of the address attempted.
  • Signature obtained of servee if able to obtain.
  • Bodycam video evidence of the service.
